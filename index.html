Hi! I'm Kristian Paul

A 3rd-year BSIT student with a passion for both the lens and the code. When I'm not studying Web Systems and Technologies, you can find me behind the camera working as a freelance videographer and photographer.

I am currently a proud member of BASTE TV, where I hone my skills in media production. My goal is to merge my technical IT background with my creative eye to build engaging digital experiences. When the work is done, I recharge by diving into the world of online gaming.

About Me

For me, development and design go hand in hand. While my academic focus is on building robust systems using C# and SQL, my background in media production has taught me that functionality means nothing without a great user experience.

Whether I am debugging a backend database or color-grading a video project, I am driven by the same goal: precision. I don't just want to write code that works; I want to build applications that feel intuitive and look professional. Currently, I am expanding my mobile development skills in Android Studio while continuing to capture stories through my freelance work.
