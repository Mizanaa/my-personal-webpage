3rd-year BSIT student with a passion for both the lens and the code. When I'm not studying Web Systems and Technologies, you can find me behind the camera working as a freelance videographer and photographer.

About Me

Whether I am debugging a backend database or color-grading a video project, I am driven by the same goal: precision. I don't just want to write code that works; I want to build applications that feel intuitive and look professional. Currently, I am expanding my mobile development skills in Android Studio while continuing to capture stories through my freelance work.
